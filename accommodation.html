The workshop will take place at Instituto de Lingüística, called Casa Lago (Dr. Manuel Albo 2663), in the neighborhood Parque Batlle.

There are different options for accommodation. The nicest neighbourhoods within the surroundings of the workshop are Pocitos, Punta Carretas and Parque Rodó. Staying anywhere situated by the coast (Uruguayans call it rambla) or in any of the streets nearby, is always a nice option.
